stratum

single player chess (against the engine) with a small twist!?

each separate level challenges you against an increasingly difficult starting position. each level contains three difficulties:

  • Beginner (★)
  • Intermediate (★★)
  • Advanced (★★★)

either draw or win the game to succeed! (some extra lenience!)

select the level you wish to play using the level select menu

you can earn up to 6 stars on each level, 3 from the engine difficulty, and 3 from the unique challenges. the next level is only unlocked when you beat the previous level, at any difficulty.

seems difficult? keep in mind that there are difficulty modifiers implemented in game

use the side console to toggle game modifiers before clicking into the match. each modifier has its own difficulty multiplier associated with it. certain modifiers impose a hard restriction preventing you from choosing certain other modifiers.

challenges listed under each level require the specific difficulty and modifiers active / overall difficulty multiplier required, in any successful attempt

meet challenge requirements and pass the level to earn the special challenge star. challenges must be followed STRICTLY to earn a completion (no extra modifiers, even if it makes it harder!)

keep in mind that it is ILLEGAL to capture the opponent's king even if your combination of modifiers allows you to do so
